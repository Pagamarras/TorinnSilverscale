Who is Torinn Silverscale?


Greetings, my name is Torinn Silverscale. Son of Dorinn Silverscale, of the House Silverscale. I'm a noble Dragonborn from a far away land called Hontharim. My father is Count Dorinn.


In Hontharim we worship the god Tyr. It is a common in our culture that a Dragonborn of noble ancestry, can be inducted to the hall of honor. To achieve this, one must complete 3 grand trails.
